Straight Pool (14.1) Scoring

The easy way to score straight pool. No counting shots, no guessing.

  1. Run your balls.
  2. When your inning ends, count whatâ€™s still on the table.
  3. Enter that number in Balls left.

Your run is scored automatically.

Re-rack

  • When only one object ball remains on the table and 14 balls are re-racked, press New rack.

Quick helpers

  • No ball made? Just hit Swap turn.
  • Scratch or foul? Toggle Error before entering balls left.
  • Fat-fingered it? Undo / Redo has you covered.

Let the table do the math.
